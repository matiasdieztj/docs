## Getting Started

Follow these steps to go from sign-up to fully operational in just a few minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Go to [puntodiez.com/signup](https://puntodiez.com/signup) and register with your name, email address, and a secure password. Check your inbox to verify your email before continuing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your workspace">
    A workspace is your team's shared environment in Punto Diez. Give it a name that reflects your organization or team, then choose the plan that fits your needs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your first project">
    Inside your workspace, click **New Project**, enter a title and description, and set a due date if needed. Your project is ready to receive tasks right away.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite your team members">
    Head to **Settings → Team** and invite colleagues by email. Assign roles to control what each person can view and edit within your workspace.
  </Step>
</Steps>
