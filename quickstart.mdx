This guide walks you through everything you need to go from zero to a fully running project in Punto Diez. By the end, you'll have an account, a workspace, your first project, and your team on board — all in just a few minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Go to [puntodiez.com/signup](https://puntodiez.com/signup) and fill in your name, email address, and a secure password.

    After submitting the form, check your inbox for a verification email. Click the confirmation link to activate your account and proceed to your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your first workspace">
    Once you're logged in, you'll be prompted to create a workspace. Enter a name for it — this is usually your company or team name.

    Select the plan that fits your needs. You can start with the free plan and upgrade at any time from **Settings → Billing**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your first project">
    From the workspace dashboard, click **New Project**. Fill in the project title and an optional description so your team understands its purpose.

    Set a start date and due date if relevant, then click **Create Project**. Your project is live and ready for tasks.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite your team">
    Navigate to **Settings → Team** and click **Invite Members**. Enter your teammates' email addresses and assign each one a role — Admin, Member, or Viewer.

    Each invitee receives an email with a link to join your workspace. They'll be active collaborators as soon as they accept.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect an integration (optional)">
    To connect an external tool, go to **Settings → Integrations**, browse the available integrations, and click **Connect** next to the service you want to use.

    Follow the authorization steps to link your account. Once connected, the integration is available across all projects in your workspace.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Make Your First API Call

Once your account is set up, you can interact with Punto Diez programmatically using the REST API. The example below fetches all projects in your workspace.

```bash
curl -X GET https://api.puntodiez.com/v1/projects \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY"
```

Replace `YOUR_API_KEY` with your actual key. The response returns a JSON array of your workspace's projects.

<Tip>
  Find your API key in **Settings → API Keys**. You can generate multiple keys for different environments or integrations and revoke them individually at any time.
</Tip>
