Punto Diez is a project and workflow management platform designed to help you organize work, align your team, and connect the tools you already use. It gives you a single, structured environment where projects move forward, nothing slips through the cracks, and everyone stays on the same page.

## What You Can Do

Punto Diez covers the full lifecycle of your work — from planning to execution to integration with external services.

## How It Works

Punto Diez is built around a simple, repeatable workflow that scales with your team.

**Create a workspace.** Your workspace is the top-level container for all your team's projects and settings. Set it up once and invite your collaborators.

**Add your projects.** Inside the workspace, create projects to represent initiatives, products, or any body of work. Each project holds tasks, files, and activity history.

**Collaborate with your team.** Assign tasks, leave comments, update statuses, and track who's doing what — all without leaving the platform.

**Integrate your tools.** Connect third-party services through the Integrations panel to automate data flow and reduce context switching across your stack.
