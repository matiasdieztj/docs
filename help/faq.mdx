Got questions? You're in the right place. This page covers the most common things our users ask about — from account basics and billing details to data handling and integrations. If you don't find what you're looking for here, our support team is always ready to help.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I reset my password?">
    If you've forgotten your password or want to change it, go to the Punto Diez login page and click **Forgot password?** below the sign-in form. Enter the email address associated with your account and click **Send Reset Link**. You'll receive an email with a link to create a new password — the link is valid for 24 hours.

    If you're already logged in, you can change your password from **Profile → Security → Change Password**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I be in multiple workspaces?">
    Yes. A single Punto Diez account can be a member of any number of workspaces. Each workspace is independent, with its own projects, team, settings, and subscription.

    To switch between workspaces, click your workspace name in the top-left corner of the dashboard and select another workspace from the dropdown, or click **Create or join a workspace** to add a new one.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens to my data if I cancel?">
    When you cancel your paid subscription, your workspace automatically reverts to the Free plan at the end of your current billing period. Your data — projects, tasks, comments, and files — is preserved in full.

    If your workspace exceeds Free plan limits, projects above the limit are archived and become read-only until you upgrade again. **Your data is never automatically deleted due to a plan cancellation.**

    If you choose to delete your workspace entirely, that action is permanent and irreversible.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I export my projects?">
    You can export your entire workspace data, including all projects and tasks, from **Settings → Data → Export**. Choose either JSON (for structured data) or CSV (for spreadsheet use), click **Export**, and wait for an email with your download link. The link is valid for 48 hours.

    For individual project exports, open the project, click the **⋯ menu** in the top-right corner, and select **Export Project**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I change my workspace URL?">
    Yes, you can change your workspace URL slug from **Settings → General → URL Slug**. Enter your new slug and click **Save Changes**.

    Keep in mind that changing your URL slug will immediately break any previously shared links to your workspace. We recommend updating your bookmarks and notifying your team after making this change.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a mobile app?">
    Yes! Punto Diez has native apps for both **iOS** and **Android**. You can download them from the [App Store](https://apple.com/app-store) or [Google Play](https://play.google.com). The mobile app supports task management, notifications, comments, and viewing project boards and timelines.

    Some advanced settings and administrative features are only available on the desktop web app.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I contact support?">
    There are several ways to reach the Punto Diez support team:

    - **In-app chat** — Click the **?** icon in the bottom-right corner of the dashboard to start a live chat.
    - **Email** — Send a message to [support@puntodiez.com](mailto:support@puntodiez.com).
    - **Help Center** — Browse articles and guides at [help.puntodiez.com](https://help.puntodiez.com).

    Pro and Enterprise customers receive priority support with faster response times.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use Punto Diez offline?">
    Punto Diez requires an internet connection for most features since your data is synced in real time across all devices and team members. However, the mobile app supports **limited offline mode** — you can view recently accessed tasks and add comments that will sync automatically once you reconnect.

    Full offline support for the desktop web app is on our product roadmap.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What integrations are available on the Free plan?">
    Free plan workspaces have access to a **basic set of integrations**, including:

    - Google Calendar (read-only sync)
    - Slack (notifications only)
    - Zapier (limited to 3 active Zaps)

    Upgrading to the **Pro plan** unlocks all integrations, including GitHub, Jira, Notion, HubSpot, and the full Zapier and Webhooks suite. See the [Integrations guide](/guides/integrations) for a complete list.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I transfer workspace ownership?">
    Only the current Owner can transfer ownership to another workspace member.

    1. Navigate to **Settings → Team**.
    2. Find the member you want to make the new Owner.
    3. Click the **role badge** next to their name and select **Transfer Ownership**.
    4. Confirm the action in the dialog. You will be downgraded to **Admin** automatically.

    Ownership transfer is immediate. Make sure the recipient is aware before initiating the transfer.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
