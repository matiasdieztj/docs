The Punto Diez dashboard is the first thing you see when you log in, and it's designed to give you an at-a-glance picture of everything happening across your workspaces and projects. From here you can navigate to any part of the platform, act on new updates, and keep your most important work just one click away. Spend a few minutes getting familiar with the layout and you'll move through your day significantly faster.

## Dashboard Layout

The dashboard is divided into three main areas:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Sidebar" icon="sidebar">
    The left-hand sidebar is your primary navigation. It lists your workspaces, pinned projects, and links to global views like **My Tasks**, **Inbox**, and **Analytics**. You can collapse the sidebar to reclaim screen space at any time.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Main Content Area" icon="layout-grid">
    The center of the screen displays the currently selected view — your home feed, a project board, a task list, or a settings page. This area adapts to whatever you're working on.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Top Bar" icon="search">
    The top bar gives you persistent access to **global search** (find any task, project, or member instantly), **notifications** (a bell icon showing unread activity), and your **profile menu** (account settings, organization switching, and sign-out).
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Activity Feed

The activity feed sits on your dashboard home and shows real-time updates for every project and task you're involved in. New comments, status changes, assignments, and due-date edits all surface here as they happen so you never miss a development.

**Filtering the feed** keeps noise to a minimum. Use the filter controls above the feed to narrow updates by:

- **Workspace** — see activity only from a specific team or business unit
- **Project** — focus on a single project's timeline of events
- **Update type** — show only assignments, comments, or status changes

Click any feed item to jump directly to the relevant task or project without losing your place.

## Quick Actions

You don't need to navigate deep into a workspace or project to create new work. The **Quick Actions** button (the `+` icon in the top bar) lets you act immediately from anywhere on the dashboard:

- **New Project** — choose a workspace, enter a name and description, and optionally set a due date to get started in seconds.
- **New Task** — select a project, write the task title, assign it to a member, and set a due date — all without leaving the current view.
- **Invite Member** — enter an email address, choose a workspace, and assign a role to bring a new collaborator on board right away.

<Note>
  Dashboard preferences — including your sidebar order, pinned projects, and default view mode — are saved per-user. Changes you make do not affect what your teammates see on their own dashboards.
</Note>

## Customizing Your View

Punto Diez lets you shape the dashboard to match the way you actually work.

**Reorder sidebar items** by dragging and dropping workspaces and projects into the sequence that makes the most sense for you. Put the teams you visit most at the top.

**Pin favorite projects** by right-clicking any project in the sidebar and selecting **Pin to Sidebar**. Pinned projects appear in a dedicated section at the top of the sidebar for instant access.

**Switch between list and board views** using the view toggle in the top-right corner of any project. The **list view** shows tasks in a structured, sortable table. The **board view** displays tasks as cards arranged in status columns — ideal for visualizing flow and spotting bottlenecks at a glance. Punto Diez remembers which view you last used for each project.
