Punto Diez offers flexible pricing plans designed to grow with your team. Whether you're a solo user exploring the Free plan or a large organization on Enterprise, your billing settings give you full visibility into your subscription, payment methods, and invoice history. All billing is managed from **Settings → Billing**.

<Info>
  Billing in Punto Diez is **workspace-level**, not per individual user account. Your subscription is tied to the workspace, and the workspace Owner is responsible for all billing activity.
</Info>

## Available Plans

Choose the plan that fits your team's size and needs. All plans include access to the core Punto Diez dashboard.

| Plan | Price | Key Features |
|---|---|---|
| **Free** | $0 / mo | Up to 3 projects, 5 team members, 1 workspace, basic integrations |
| **Pro** | $12 / user / mo | Unlimited projects, guest access, all integrations, priority support |
| **Enterprise** | Custom pricing | SSO & SAML, advanced permissions, audit logs, dedicated support manager |

Contact [sales@puntodiez.com](mailto:sales@puntodiez.com) to request an Enterprise quote or a custom demo.

## Upgrading Your Plan

Upgrading takes effect immediately — your team gains access to new features as soon as the payment is confirmed.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Billing Settings">
    Go to **Settings → Billing** and click **Upgrade Plan**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a Plan">
    Review the available plans and click **Select** next to the one you want. You will see a cost estimate based on your current seat count.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Payment Details">
    Enter your credit card information or select a saved card from your payment methods.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Your Upgrade">
    Click **Confirm Upgrade**. You will receive a confirmation email with your first invoice attached.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Upgrading mid-billing cycle? Punto Diez automatically prorates your charge so you only pay for the remaining days in the current period.
</Tip>

## Managing Payment Methods

You can store multiple credit or debit cards on your workspace and designate one as the default for recurring charges.

**To add a new card:**
1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Payment Methods**.
2. Click **Add Payment Method**.
3. Enter your card details and click **Save Card**.

**To set a default card:**
1. Find the card you want to use as default.
2. Click the **⋯ menu** next to it.
3. Select **Set as Default**.

**To remove a card:**
1. Click the **⋯ menu** next to the card.
2. Select **Remove**. You cannot remove the default card while an active subscription is running — set another card as default first.

## Invoices

Punto Diez generates an invoice automatically at the start of each billing cycle. All past invoices are available for download at any time.

1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Invoices**.
2. Find the invoice you need in the list, sorted by date.
3. Click **Download PDF** to save a copy for your records.

Invoices include a full itemized breakdown of your subscription, seat count, any add-ons, and applied discounts or credits.

## Canceling or Downgrading

**To downgrade to the Free plan:**
1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Change Plan**.
2. Select **Free** and confirm the downgrade.
3. Your Pro or Enterprise features will remain active until the end of the current billing period.

**To cancel your subscription:**
1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Cancel Subscription**.
2. Review the cancellation summary and click **Confirm Cancellation**.
3. Your workspace will revert to the Free plan at the end of the billing period. Your data is retained.

<Warning>
  If your workspace exceeds the Free plan limits at the time of downgrade (more than 3 projects or 5 members), **the oldest projects above the limit will be archived** and become read-only. No data is deleted, but you will need to upgrade again to regain full access. Remove excess projects or members before downgrading to avoid this.
</Warning>
